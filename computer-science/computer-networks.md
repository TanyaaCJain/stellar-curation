## RFC Series - [RFC Editor](https://www.rfc-editor.org/)
**Read the internet's source code.** The RFC (Request for Comments) series is the canonical archive where our digital world was drafted. It started in 1969 with a single informal memo for ARPANET developers and evolved into the official record of the internet itself. This is the raw, unedited history, the original proposals and debates that became the protocols powering everything today.

For a masterclass in systems thinking, explore these documents. You're reading the original specs from the visionaries who coded our connected reality. Maintained by the RFC Editor, this living library continues to shape the future of the internet. Dive in and see how it all connects.
